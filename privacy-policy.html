Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

Forged 365 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Name and email address (when you create an account)
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)

App Usage Data:

  • Devotional progress and journal entries
  • Fitness workout logs and preferences
  • Financial data you enter (budget, expenses, debts, savings goals, giving logs)
  • Onboarding preferences (age range, goals, fitness level)

Device Information:

  • Push notification tokens (for sending daily reminders)
  • Device type and operating system
  • Language preference

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the app experience
  • To track your devotional, fitness, and financial progress
  • To send daily devotional reminders (push notifications, with your permission)
  • To send password reset emails
  • To manage your subscription
  • To generate your Kingdom Insights reports

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted cloud servers. Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt). We use HTTPS for all data transmission. We do not store your payment information — all subscription payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store via RevenueCat.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo — Push notification delivery (Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. Your journal entries, fitness logs, and financial data are private to your account.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data within the app
  • Update your account information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time via device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Forged 365 is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
